\chapter{ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS}

\begin{singlespace}
\noindent
Acknowledgement is a statement of gratitude for assistance in producing a work. Usually the first person you have to acknowledge is your main supervisor or Professor. Follow by your co-supervisor. You may also include those who have help or assist you in completing your research e.g. other Professors, lab technician, lab members etc.\\

\noindent
Other than that, authors also often thank their family, wife, children or maybe friends in their acknowledgement. Anyhow acknowledgement is a part when you would like to express to sincere gratitude to those who helped you in completing your work.
